    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are 5 professional FAQ questions and answers tailored for our comapany,NODTECH Engineering. These questions are realistic and client-focused, addressing common concerns about our services, processes and supports we provide.

    1. What services does NODTECH Engineering provide?

    We specialize in full-stack software development, offering services such as website and mobile app development, custom software solutions, cloud deployment, and system integration. We also provide tech training and consultancy for businesses looking to modernize their digital infrastructure.

    2. How long does it take to build a website or application?

    Project timelines depend on the complexity and features required. A basic company website can take 1–2 weeks, while custom applications with complex backend systems may take 4–8 weeks or more. We provide a clear timeline after assessing your requirements.

    3. Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

    Yes. We offer continuous support and maintenance services to ensure your system runs smoothly after launch. This includes bug fixes, updates, feature enhancements, and security patches based on the agreed service level.

    4. Can I request changes during development?

    Absolutely. We maintain an agile approach, allowing client feedback at each stage of development. We schedule checkpoints where we review progress together, and adjust based on your evolving needs.

    5. How secure is the software or website you build?

    Security is a top priority. We implement best practices like HTTPS, data validation, secure authentication, and protection against common threats (e.g., SQL injection, CSRF, and XSS). We also recommend periodic security audits and updates for optimal protection.
